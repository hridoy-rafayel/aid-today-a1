Monetary donation

Sending money is an easy and effective way to help. Donate money to NGOs mounting relief response. Support an NGO by volunteering on the ground. Donate in kind (medicines, blankets, food items, etc.)

Donate goods.

Gently used clothing, socks, shoes, bedding, and blankets are almost always in need after a devastating flood. First aid kits, tents, mosquito netting, soap, and hygiene products might also be needed. Purchase and donate new, non-perishable food items and bottled drinking water.

Work as a Volunteer

If it's safe enough to travel to the afflicted area, see if aid organizations are looking for volunteers to help on the ground. Consider volunteering with Habitat for Humanities or other organizations whose mission is to clear debris
